Delicious and simple pizza

With this simple recipe everyone will be able to make a pizza at home like a true Italian!

Dough ingredients:

  • Flour - 500g
  • Water - 400g
  • Extra virgin olive oil - 20g
  • Salt - 10g
  • Yeast - 1tbs.

Toppings ingredients

  • Tomato sauce - 500g
  • Grated mozzarella - 250g

Steps:

  1. Add flour, oil, salt and yeast in a bowl and mix them with a spoon.
  2. Add the water to the mix and mix with a spoon until you get a rough dough.
  3. Wait 15 minutes.
  4. After 15 minutes knead the dough with your hands until the dough becomes smooth.
  5. Cover with plastic wrap and leave in the fridge for 12 hours.
  6. After 12 hours remove dough from the fridge and put it on an oiled tray.
  7. Turn on over at 180 degrees Celsius.
  8. Put the tomato sauce and mozzarella on the dough.
  9. Put the pizza in the oven for ca. 20/30minutes.
  10. Your pizza is ready!
