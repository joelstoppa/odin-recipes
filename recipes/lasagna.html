Lasagna recipe

Ragu ingredients:

  • Ground meat - 500g
  • Carrots - 50g
  • Onions - 50g
  • Cellery - 50g
  • Tomato puree - 500g
  • Red wine - 1 glass
  • Olive oil - 3 spoons
  • Salt and pepper - as needed

Besciamella ingredients:

  • Butter - 70g
  • Milk - 1 liter
  • Flour - 70g
  • Nutmeg - as needed
  • Salt - as needed

Additional ingredients:

  • Parmigiano Reggiano DOP - 250g
  • Lasagna sheets

Ragu steps:

  1. Finely chop carrots, cellery and onions.
  2. In a large pot add chopped ingredients with the olive oil and let cook until golden.
  3. Add ground meat and let cook until browned.
  4. Add red wine and let completely evaporate.
  5. Add tomato puree, salt and pepper.
  6. Let simmer on low heat for at least 3 hours.

Besciamella steps:

  1. Add milk in a pot and heat without boiling.
  2. In a separate pot add butter and flour and let cook a few minutes.
  3. Add salt and nutmeg to the milk
  4. Add the hot milk to the cooked butter and flour mixture and mix until no lump are present.

Assembly steps:

  1. Take 30x20 cm tray.
  2. Add 1 layer of besciamella.
  3. Add 1 layer of ragu.
  4. Add 1 thin layer of ground Parmigiano Reggiano.
  5. Add 1 layer of lasagna sheets.
  6. Repeat previous steps until the tray is filled.
  7. Add a generous layer of ground Parmigiano Reggiano on top.
  8. Cook in a preheated oven at 180Â° for around 40 minutes.
  9. Your lasagna is ready to be enjoyed!
